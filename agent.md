# Project Brief: PILGRIM - Comptoir Végétal & Concept Store

## 1. Project Overview
**PILGRIM** is a hybrid destination located in Pézenas, France, blending a "cuisine vivante" (living kitchen) with a curated boutique of artisanal objects. The project is rooted in the "Slow Life" philosophy, emphasizing quality, short supply chains, and well-being.

## 2. Brand Identity & Tone
- **Brand Name**: PILGRIM
- **Core Concept**: Comptoir Végétal & Concept Store
- **Slogan**: Slow Life, Slow Food, Vegetarian Food
- **Values**: 100% Plant-based, Short Circuit, Quality, Kindness
- **Tone**: Authentic, welcoming, professional, and wellness-oriented.
- **Visual Style**: High-contrast (Slate black vs. Electric blue), artisanal, elegant, and "Slow Life" aesthetic.

- **Address**: 11, rue Aristide Rouzière, 34120 Pézenas

## 4. Product Offerings
### The "Comptoir" (Catering)
- **Signature Dish**: "Good Karma" Menu (100% Veggie) - 16.90€.
- **Drinks**: Extensive list including local wines, craft beers, botanical lemonades, and specialty lattes (Golden/Matcha).
- **Desserts**: Artisanal cakes and fruit bowls.

### The "Concept Store" (Boutique)
- **Terroir Products**: Fine grocery, oils, honey, and local infusions.
- **Decoration**: Handcrafted ceramics, linen household items.
- **Wellness**: Natural skincare, botanical candles, and seasonal rituals.

## 5. Design Requirements (Landing Page)
- **One-Page Navigation**: Smooth scrolling between sections.
- **Header**: Text logo (PILGRIM), navigation (Comptoir, Concept Store, Slow Life, Contact).
- **Hero Section**: Authentic storefront imagery with clear "Slow Life" messaging.
- **Menu Section**: Transcription of the handwritten chalkboard "Carte du Moment" alongside original photography.
- **Visual Cues**: Use of iconic blue chairs and chalkboard textures to maintain establishment personality.
- **Responsive**: Full mobile optimization.

## 6. Target Audience
Locals and visitors in Pézenas seeking high-quality vegetarian food, artisanal home goods, and a mindful shopping/dining experience.
